**Unit 06: Merchandising Operations**

- Calculating the net cost of an inventory purchase
- Journal entry for buyer transactions under a perpetual inventory system, gross method: purchase
- Journal entries for seller transactions under a perpetual inventory, system, gross method: sale
- Merchandising formulas: net sales, net purchases, cost of goods sold, and gross profit

**Unit 07: Cash and Cash Equivalents**

- Calculating cash and cash equivalents
    - cash: currency and coin, petty cash, all bank accounts, all checks not yet deposited, deposits in transit
    - cash equivalents: all investments maturing within 90 days
- Bank reconciliation:
    - Reconciling items on a bank reconciliation: added to bank balance, subtracted from bank balance, added to book balance, or subtracted from bank balance
    - Calculating adjusted balance per bank
    - Calculating adjusted balance per books
- Petty cash: Journal entries to establish/raise/reduce petty cash fund

**Unit 08: Merchandise Inventory**

- What is included in inventory: physical count, goods in transit, consigned goods
    - Freight terms, inventory cut-off for goods in transit: include in inventory purchases made FOB shipping point and sales made FOB destination
- Inventory costing methods: computing cost of goods sold and ending inventory under a periodic inventory system using (1) FIFO, (2) LIFO, and (3) weighted average cost
- Income effects of inventory costing methods
- Inventory reporting: calculating the inventory reported on a balance sheet based on the lower of cost or market valuation technique
